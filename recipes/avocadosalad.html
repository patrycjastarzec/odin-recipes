Avocado salad

Description

Light and fresh avocado salad with lettuce, tomato, cucumber and onion. Perfect for lunch, easy to prepare and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 lettuce
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 fresh cucumber
  • 1 red onion

Steps

  1. Wash the vegetables
  2. Peel and dice avocado
  3. Slice cucumber
  4. Dice tomato
  5. Chop red onion
  6. Tear lettuce into small pieces
  7. Mix the ingredients

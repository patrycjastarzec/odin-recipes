Omelette

Description

An omelette is perfect for breakfast, it is easy to made and fast to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tomato
  • a few basil leaves
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tbs of oil

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl and mix
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Add basil leaves
  4. Dice tomato and add to the eggs
  5. Pour oil into the pan and then add eggs
  6. Fry until the omelette is ready

Baked apple

Description

Baked apple is perfect for breakfast or lunch. It is sweet and full of cinnamon. Great for a fall season.

Ingredients

  • 1 apple
  • 1 tbs of cinnamon
  • 1 tbs of cardamoms
  • 1 tbs of brown sugar
  • 1 tbs of honey

Steps

  1. Cut the apple in half and remove the seeds
  2. Sprinkle with spices and sugar
  3. Put it into oven and bake it for 15 minutes in 180 degrees
  4. Pour over the honey
